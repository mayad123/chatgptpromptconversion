import pandas as pd

# 2. Feature engineering

Why Optimize Your Prompts?

Prompt optimization is the art and science of crafting effective instructions for AI models. Here's why it matters and how it transforms your interactions with ChatGPT.

Better Accuracy & Relevance

Well-optimized prompts provide clear context and specific instructions, leading to more accurate and relevant responses. The AI understands exactly what you need rather than guessing from vague requests.

Improved Efficiency

Optimized prompts reduce the need for multiple iterations and clarifications. You get the desired output faster, saving time and API costs. One well-crafted prompt can replace several back-and-forth exchanges.

Enhanced Quality

By following best practices like role assignment, clear structure, and output format specification, you guide the AI to produce higher-quality, more consistent results that meet your specific needs.

Reduced Ambiguity

Vague prompts lead to unpredictable results. Optimization eliminates ambiguity by providing clear instructions, context, and constraints, ensuring the AI understands your intent precisely.

Professional Output

Structured prompts with role assignments and formatting guidelines help the AI produce professional, polished content that matches your requirements, tone, and style.

Consistency

Using optimized prompt templates ensures consistent results across multiple interactions. Once you've optimized a prompt, you can reuse it reliably for similar tasks.

Key Best Practices

1. Be Clear and Specific

Instead of: "Tell me about AI"
Use: "Explain the key principles of artificial intelligence and its applications in healthcare, suitable for a beginner audience."

2. Assign Roles

Give ChatGPT a specific role or persona to guide the response style. Example: "You are an expert educator explaining complex topics to beginners."

3. Provide Context

Include relevant background information and constraints. Context helps the AI understand your situation and tailor responses accordingly.

4. Specify Output Format

Clearly define how you want the output structured. Specify format, length, style, and any specific elements you need included.

5. Use Delimiters

Separate different sections of your prompt using delimiters (like """ or ###) to help the AI distinguish between instructions and input data.

6. Iterate and Refine

Start with a basic prompt, review the output, and refine it based on results. Prompt engineering is an iterative process that improves with practice.
